PPE Detection

An AI-based computer vision system developed to monitor workplace safety by detecting the presence of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) such as helmets, safety vests, and masks in real time. The system was built using the pre-trained YOLOv8 model, which I further fine-tuned by training on a customized dataset to improve detection accuracy in industrial environments. By analyzing live video streams, the model ensures compliance with safety regulations and helps reduce occupational hazards through intelligent, real-time monitoring.

Fire Detection

An AI-powered computer vision system designed to detect fire and smoke in real time using deep learning techniques. The model was developed and trained using TensorFlow and Keras, where I built and fine-tuned a convolutional neural network to accurately recognize early signs of fire hazards. By analyzing live video streams, the system triggers instant alerts to help prevent large-scale damage and enhance safety in residential and industrial environments.

Fatigue and Sleep Detection

An intelligent deep learning-based computer vision system for real-time driver alertness monitoring, developed by fine-tuning a pretrained YOLOv8n model for face and eye detection. The system analyzes eye movements, blink rate, and facial cues to detect signs of drowsiness and trigger timely alerts, improving road safety and reducing accident risks.
